Will Miner

Hi, I'm Will Miner, a designer and product leader based in New York.

I'm a design leader focused on building tools that help people think, collaborate, and get work done. Currently, I lead design at Slack across platform, automation, and AI.

My background spans product design, engineering, and organizational leadership. I care about usability, efficiency, and the tiny details that add up to a pleasant experience. Over the past fifteen years, I've worked across startups and large platforms, often in ambiguous, emerging problem spaces.

Outside of work, I enjoy trail running, indoor gardening, fly fishing, art history, vibe coding, and electronic music. I currently live in the Hudson Valley of New York.

Will Miner

Hi, I'm Will Miner, a designer and
product leader based in New York.

I build tools that help people think, communicate, and get things done. Most recently at Slack, I lead design across automation and AI. My work lives at the intersection of human systems and software systems, where a small change in interface can shift how teams collaborate or how someone feels about their own competence.

I've spent the last fifteen years moving between design, code, and leadership, chasing clarity and craft in all three. I like work that rewards patience and asks for care. Outside of it, I write, build furniture, and tend to a garden that's always halfway wild.

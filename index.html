Will Miner

Hi, I'm Will Miner. I lead product design at Slack, where my team works on platform, AI, and the tools that help people and agents get work done together. Over fifteen years I've led design and product teams at Slack, 2U, and Intel, moving through EdTech, semiconductors, and enterprise software along the way.

I've spent a lot of time thinking about where design adds real value to a business That's part of why I got an MBA while working full-time, and why I keep coming back to questions about craft, leadership, and what it takes to build a team that can sustain good work.

Outside of work I fly fish, tend a garden, build software for fun, and think maybe too hard about where technology is actually going.
